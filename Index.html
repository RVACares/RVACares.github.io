How we came about!!!


There are many well known charity organizations in the United States. Most of them hire top notch employess to run them. As a result 50-95% of donations are used to run these companies rather than helping people. Thats where RVAcares comes in. It is a organization run by all the members of the RVA society. There are no CEO's or employees with extensive training. It is just people trying help people.

What we believe in!!!


We believe in helping people, those who are struggling to make ends meet. RVA cares is a way to provide them support and atleast get some of mind. This website can help our veterans, our homeless, students, single parents etc

Our Policies!!


We encourage all our RVA memebers to post only gently used items online. Please keep in mind that this website is made to share with your fellow citizens. They desrve to respected regardless their finacial condition. Please do not make them feel discouraged by giving damaged goods or even unclean items. Make sure your item are well cleaned and washed, even sanitized and ready to use.
